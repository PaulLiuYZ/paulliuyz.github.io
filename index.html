Biography

Yuzhou Liu is pusuring his Phd degree in the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences under the supervision of Professor Shuhan Shen since September, 2021. He also works closely with Dr. Lingjie Zhu and Professor Xiang Gao. Prior to that, he received the B.E. degree in the School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering from Shanghai JiaoTong University in 2021, ranking 2/61. His research interests lie in the area of computer vision, specifically focusing on 3D vision including 3D reconstruction, generative model, and multimodel understanding. Always feel free to contact me if you have any question or want to cooperate with me!
