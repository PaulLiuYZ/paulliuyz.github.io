Short Bio
Yuzhou Liu is pusuring his Phd degree in the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences since September, 2021. Prior to that, he received the B.S. degree in the School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering from Shanghai JiaoTong University in 2021, ranking 2/61. His research interests lie in the area of computer vision, specifically focusing on 3D vision including vectorized modeling, floorplan reconstruction and generative model.
